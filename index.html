About

I'm a UI/UX Designer with 7 years of experience across healthcare, HR tech, and digital services. I work across the full design process — research, interaction design, prototyping, testing, and working closely with developers to make sure things ship the way they were designed.

Before moving into UX, I spent several years in digital marketing. That background gives me a different lens: I think about users, but I also think about why a product exists and what it needs to do for the business. I find that combination useful, especially in complex domains where the problem is rarely just a UI problem.

Start with the problem
Good design starts before the first wireframe. I spend time understanding what's actually going wrong before deciding what to build.
Design for the edge case
The average user flow is easy. The interesting — and important — work is in the moments when things don't go as expected.
Stay close to the build
I work closely with developers throughout delivery. A design that doesn't survive implementation isn't a good design.
Show the thinking
I document decisions, not just outcomes. Understanding why a design choice was made is as important as the choice itself.
